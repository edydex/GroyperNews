About Us

Welcome to Groyper News, your independent source for the latest news, incisive analysis, and thoughtful commentary on the stories that shape our world. Based on the West Coast, we are an independently owned media outlet dedicated to delivering news that is accurate, unfiltered, and free from corporate interference.

At Groyper News, we believe that journalism should empower people, not corporations. Our team of experienced journalists works around the clock to provide our audience with clear, reliable reporting on both local and national events. We focus on delivering content that challenges the status quo, questions the narratives pushed by mainstream outlets, and highlights the issues that truly matter.

Founded in 2025, Groyper News stands as a beacon of independent journalism. We are committed to offering stories and perspectives often overlooked by traditional media, giving voice to diverse viewpoints and championing truth in an era of misinformation. Our goal is simple: to inform, engage, and inspire our readers to think critically and act with purpose.

Thank you for choosing Groyper News. Your trust allows us to continue our mission of providing honest, thoughtful, and independent reporting.
